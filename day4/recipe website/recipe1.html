Fruit nachos

Fruit nachos are a delicious and fun way to enjoy fruit as a snack or dessert. They consist of a layer of baked or air-fried cinnamon sugar tortilla chips with slices of fresh fruit, such as apples, strawberries, bananas, kiwi, and pineapple, arranged on a plate or platter and topped with a variety of toppings, such as nuts, chocolate chips, shredded coconut, granola, yogurt, whipped cream, and ice cream.Fruit nachos can be served as a snack or dessert, either alone or as part of a larger meal. The toppings can also be customized to suit individual tastes, with a variety of fruits, nuts, and other sweet treats available to choose from. Fruit nachos are a great way to enjoy the natural sweetness of fresh fruit while also adding a little extra flavor and fiber and texture to the mix with the tortillas.

Ingredients

Carb Balance Flour Tortillas
Butter, melted
Cooking Spray (use avocado oil spray or olive oil spray with no propellants)
Granulated Sugar
Cinnamon
Strawberries, sliced
Blueberries
Kiwi, diced
Mango, diced
Pistachios, chopped
Light Vanilla Ice Cream and/or Light Whipped Cream
